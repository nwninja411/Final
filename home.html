A little about me

Hi, I'm Noah! Before taking this class, I had absolutely no coding experience. My goal in enrolling in this Introduction to Programming course was to improve my ability to write expressions for Adobe After Effects and Premiere Pro, tools I regularly use for video editing and motion design. I hoped that understanding coding principles would help streamline my workflow and open up new possibilities in the creative projects I love. While I didn’t learn as much directly applicable to those tools as I had initially hoped, I discovered something even better—a passion for coding itself. This class became an unexpected journey into the world of programming, and it sparked a new creative outlet for me. I found joy in solving problems through logic, building projects piece by piece, and seeing how code could transform ideas into tangible results. Whether it was learning how to design websites, create simple games, or bring a touch of interactivity to my work, I quickly realized that coding wasn't just about numbers and syntax—it was a way to express creativity in a structured, yet limitless way. Now, I see coding as a skill that complements my interests in video editing and motion design, but also stands on its own as a rewarding and exciting hobby. This course has laid the foundation for me to keep exploring, learning, and growing as a coder. Whether I'm designing a visually engaging website, experimenting with animations, or solving technical challenges, I’m excited to see where this combination of creativity and logic can take me in the future.
